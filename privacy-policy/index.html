Getting in touch

When you get in touch with Jane Dearden via email, your email address will be collected. It will be used to reply to your enquiry. It will not be used to sell on to third parties or spam you with unnecessary emails.

If your enquiry results in us working together your details will be held in the company address book. If you would like to be removed from the address book you can request by emailing the Date Protection Officer.

If your enquiry does not result in us working together, your details will be deleted after 3 months.
